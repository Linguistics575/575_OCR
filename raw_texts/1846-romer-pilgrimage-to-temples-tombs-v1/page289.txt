This sweeping censure of course refers more
to the sculptures than to the architecture of the
Ptolemaic temples, for bas-reliefs and hiero-
glyphic legends were the peculiar province of
Champollion, and interested him far more than
the proportions of propyla and pronaos. Those
of Edfou are in profusion, and to my unpractised
eye appear to be beautifully executed, the greater
