I had been some time in Cairo before I dared to
mount the “ high ass ;” for their appearance is
really formidable. I adopted the plan followed by
many ladies here, that of a prayer-carpet spread on
a common saddle; but in visiting the high hareems,
it became necessary to ride the high ass; and I
found it inﬁnitely more agreeable than my usual
‘ donkey’s equipment. Certainly I was obliged con-
stantly to stoop my head under the gateways, and
came nearly in contact with some projecting ﬁrst-
floor windows; therefore I found it necessary to be
on the alert; but setting aside these objections,
there is no comparison to be made between the
“ high ass” and the ordinary donkeys—the former
is so decidedly preferable.
