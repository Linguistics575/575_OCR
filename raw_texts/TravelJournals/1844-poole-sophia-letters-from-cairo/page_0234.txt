mode of sitting at table, and it is surprising to see
how many persons can sit with comfort round a
comparatively small tray. I should advise you and
other friends in England to resume the use of
small round tables: I have often regretted they
are no longer in fashion: for a small family, they
are inﬁnitely more comfortable than the large
square or oblong tables used in England.
