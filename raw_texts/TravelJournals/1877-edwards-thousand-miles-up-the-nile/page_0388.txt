' The story of Osiris—the beneﬁcent God, the friend of man, slain
and dismembered by Typhon ; buried in a score of graves ; sought by
Isis; recovered limb by limb; resuscitated in the ﬂesh; transferred
from earth to reign over the dead in the world of Shades—is one of
the most complex of Egyptian legends. Osiris under some aspects is
the Nile. He personiﬁes Abstract Good. He appears as a Myth of
the Solar Year. He bears a notable likeness to Prometheus, and to the
