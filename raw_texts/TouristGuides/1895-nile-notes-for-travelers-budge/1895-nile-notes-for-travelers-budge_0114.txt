THE RELIGION AND GODS OF EGYPT. 91

OBasti, who comest forth from the tomb (P), I have

never eaten my heart (226., lied).

O Legs of Flame, who come forth from the darkness

of night, I have never made an attack upon any man.

0 Eater of Blood, who comest forth from the block of

sacriﬁce, I have never meditated upon iniquity.

O Eater of the intestines, who comest forth from the

Abode of the Thirty, Ihave never stolen tilled ground.

0 Lord of Law, who comest forth from the abode of

Law, I have never entered into a conspiracy.
O thou that stridest backwards, who comest forth from
Bubastis, I have never accused any man of crime.

. O Sertiu, who comest forth from Heliopolis, I have

never been angry without cause.

0 god of two-fold evil, who comest forth from the nome

Atchi,* I have never committed adultery.

O Uamemti, who comest forth from Chebt, I have

never committed adultery.

O thou that observest what hath been brought into the

Temple of Amsu, I have never deﬁled myself.

. 0 ye Chiefs, who come forth from the persea trees, I
have never caused terror.

. O Chemi, who comest forth from Ku, I have never
transgressed.

. O Reciter of words, who comest forth from Urit, I
have never spoken in hot anger.

. 0 Babe, who comest forth from Uab,1' I have never
made my ear (literally, face) deaf to the sound of
words of truth.

. O Kenememti, who comest forth from Kenemmet, I
have never uttered curses.

. O thou that bringest thy offering, who comest forth

from Seut, I have never put out my hand in a

quarrel.

* The ninth nome of Lower Egypt.
1‘ The 19th nome of Upper Egypt, capital Oxyrhynchos.
